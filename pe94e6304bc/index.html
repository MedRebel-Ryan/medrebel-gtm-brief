Why this is conservative

The model ignores three real tailwinds: the email list (~570 leads) keeps converting after the pilot ends, MedRebel OS customers can add HR at $99/mo with near-zero acquisition cost, and a single multi-location group or DSO conversion can be worth many times a solo practice. Downside case (6% lead-to-demo, 20% close) still yields ~7 customers; upside (10% / 30%) yields ~14.

Projections are estimates based on industry benchmark ranges for healthcare B2B paid media and MedRebel's existing funnel. They are illustrative, not guarantees of performance. Actual results vary with creative, targeting, and market conditions.

The Note, In Detail

How the $36,000 note works, in plain terms.

A convertible note is a loan designed to become equity, while still protecting the lender. For family, the key point is simple: you get your money back, or you get equity worth more, whichever you prefer when the time comes. There are no monthly payments in between.

No monthly installments

There are no monthly payments. The $36,000 plus 6% simple interest accrues and is settled either by converting to equity or by repayment at the next round or at maturity.

You are protected either way

At the next round you choose: take repayment of $36,000 plus accrued interest, or convert to equity (about 3.0% at the proposed cap). The valuation cap and 20% discount make conversion favorable if you want the upside.
